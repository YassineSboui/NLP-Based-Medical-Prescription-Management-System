NLP-Based Medical Prescription Management System - Use Case Tests
=================================================================

Use these examples in the Streamlit frontend or FastAPI `/analyze` endpoint.

1. Malaria
Input: I have fever, headache, chills, sweating and body pain for 3 days.
Expected: malaria

2. Typhoid fever
Input: I have prolonged high fever, fatigue, headache, nausea and abdominal pain.
Expected: typhoid fever

3. Tuberculosis
Input: I have had a cough for more than three weeks, night sweats, fever and weight loss.
Expected: tuberculosis

4. HIV
Input: I have flu-like symptoms after possible exposure, fever, sore throat, rash and swollen lymph nodes.
Expected: hiv

5. Dengue
Input: I have high fever, severe headache, pain behind the eyes, joint pain and rash.
Expected: dengue

6. Cholera
Input: I have severe watery diarrhea, vomiting, thirst and dehydration after drinking unsafe water.
Expected: cholera

7. Pneumonia
Input: I have cough, fever, chills, chest pain when breathing and shortness of breath.
Expected: pneumonia

8. Meningitis
Input: I have fever, severe headache, stiff neck, vomiting and light sensitivity.
Expected: meningitis

9. Hepatitis B
Input: I have yellow eyes, dark urine, nausea, vomiting and abdominal pain.
Expected: hepatitis b

10. Measles
Input: My child has high fever, cough, runny nose, red watery eyes and a rash spreading from the face.
Expected: measles

11. COVID-like illness
Input: I have fever, dry cough, shortness of breath, fatigue and loss of taste.
Expected: covid-like illness

12. Common cold
Input: I have runny nose, sneezing, sore throat, nasal congestion and mild cough.
Expected: common cold

13. Gastroenteritis
Input: I have diarrhea, vomiting, nausea and stomach pain after eating suspicious food.
Expected: gastroenteritis

14. Flu
Input: I suddenly developed fever, chills, cough, sore throat, muscle aches and fatigue.
Expected: flu

Negation test
Input: I have cough and chest pain but no fever and no chills.
Expected: extract cough and chest pain, but not fever or chills.
